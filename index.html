About

I am an Assistant Professor in the SQC & OR Unit at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. My research interests lie at the intersection of Survival Analysis, Reliability, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Time Series Anomaly Detection, Log Analysis, NLP, and LLMs.

Previously, I worked as a Lead Data Scientist at Ericsson India Global Services, leading machine learning and AI efforts in telecom analytics, anomaly detection, and federated learning.

I received my PhD in Mathematics from IIT Patna in 2019, where I focused on statistical inference under censoring. My current work focuses on federated learning, trustworthy AI, and scalable statistical models.
