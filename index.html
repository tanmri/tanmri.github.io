About

I am an Assistant Professor in the SQC & OR Unit at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. My research interests lie at the intersection of Survival Analysis, Reliability, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Time Series Anomaly Detection, Log Analysis, NLP, LLM.

Prior to joining ISI, I worked as a Lead Data Scientist at Ericsson India Global Services, where I led several machine learning and AI initiatives focused on telecommunications networks, anomaly detection, and federated learning systems. I have extensive experience in developing scalable ML solutions for industrial applications.

I completed my PhD in Mathematics from IIT Patna in 2019, where I worked on statistical inference and optimal life testing plans under various censoring schemes. My current research focuses on federated learning, multi-task NLP, multi-modal LLM, anomaly detection, log analysis and survival analysis for big data applications.
