About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Statistics and Operations Research (SQC & OR) Unit at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. My research interests lie at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and their applications in real-world problems.

Prior to joining ISI, I worked as a Lead Data Scientist at Ericsson India Global Services, where I led several machine learning and AI initiatives focused on telecommunications networks, anomaly detection, and federated learning systems. I have extensive experience in developing scalable ML solutions for industrial applications.

I completed my PhD in Mathematics from IIT Patna in 2019, where I worked on statistical inference and optimal experimental designs under various censoring schemes. My current research focuses on federated learning, trustworthy AI, and statistical methods for big data applications.
